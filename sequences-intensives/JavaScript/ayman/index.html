Duel Of Ki Blast

Instructions :

The game is played on the same computer and is played in 3 winning rounds.

The principle is simple: Player 1 controls Goku and Player 2 controls Vegeta.

To win, your KI must hit the opponent. To do this you need to smash your respectful keyboard keys.

If you are in trouble and the opponent takes the advantage, then you unlock new keys that have more power.

If you lose a round, you have a power bonus for the next round
Controls :

Player 1 : A > Z > E > R > T

Player 2 : Y < U < I < O < P
